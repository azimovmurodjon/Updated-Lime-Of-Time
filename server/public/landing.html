Simple Process

Book in 3 Easy Steps

Clients can book appointments in under 2 minutes — no account required.

1

Choose a Service

Browse services by category — Hair, Massage, Nails, Skincare, and more. See pricing and duration upfront.

2

Pick Date & Time

Select your preferred date from the calendar. Available time slots update in real time based on staff availability.

3

Confirm & Pay

Review your booking, apply any discounts or gift cards, choose your location, and confirm. Done!
